Over the years, I’ve had the opportunity to lead high-performing teams, mentor colleagues, and shape operational excellence across the district. Some highlights include:

  • Talent Recruitment: Bringing in top talent to strengthen district teams.
  • Customer Service Excellence: Achieving the highest customer service scores in the district.
  • Audit Success: Leading branches that passed audits with distinction.
  • Leadership Development: Building a leaders’ team to expand knowledge and performance across branches.
  • Career Growth: Advanced from Teller → Banker → Loan Officer → Senior Manager, recognized for initiative and results.

I continue to bring the same passion and commitment to every role, applying my skills to lead, mentor, and create meaningful impact wherever I work.
